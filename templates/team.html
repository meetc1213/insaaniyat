About Us

Who We Are

Insaaniyat is the Hindi word for humanity. As the name suggests this is a humanitarian effort by 2 students of the Jayshree Periwal International School aimed to satisfy the dire and fatal scarcity of plasma for COVID 19 patients by connecting them to those who have already recovered from the disease. Through social media, we hope to spread awareness regarding our website and also instigate eligible donors to donate their blood to those in need. We remind our followers that there is a finite amount of blood in this world, for them to receive it in the future someone would have to donate it first.

How We Began

When a friend of Ishaan’s contracted contacted Ishaan to donate blood for his grandmother who was ailing from COVID, but the doctors declared him unfit to do so on account of him being a minor, Ishaan was filled with a sense of impotent rage. Determined to not remain a bystander in the fight against COVID he enlisted the help of his school-mate, Meet, to establish Insaaniyat.
