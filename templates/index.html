Helping humans in the name of humanity

Your blood contains Convalescent Plasma, which can serve as the elixir of life

If you fought the infection, your plasma now contains COVID-19 antibodies. These antibodies provided one way for your immune system to fight the virus when you were sick, so your plasma may be able to be used to help others fight off the disease.

You are eligible to donate blood for covid patients if:

  • You have a test confirming that you have Covid in the past 90 days
  • You have been symptom free for atleast 14 days
  • You don't suffer from any severe pre-existing medical condition
  • You are atleast 18 years old
